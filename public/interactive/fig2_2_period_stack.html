The period stack as a vertical cross-section. Dependencies flow downward: every element in a given period depends on elements in lower periods, and nothing in a lower period depends on anything above it. This one-directional structure is what makes the period axis derivable rather than assigned. A composition that requires a P5 element necessarily requires elements from P1 through P4, whether or not the designer of that composition explicitly included them. The sub-atomic Layer 0 (hardware substrate) sits below P1 and is outside the ASM element inventory.
